The Business: "Simple & Boring"

James Latham is a classic distributor. They buy timber and panels in huge bulk from global suppliers. They store it in 12 massive depots across the UK. They sell it to joiners, construction firms, and furniture makers who need it immediately.

They make money on the spread between buying large and selling small. That's it. No complex algorithms, no hidden tech—just moving wood from where it is to where it's needed.
